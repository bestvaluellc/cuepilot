Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Overview

CuePilot respects your privacy. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use CuePilot, how that information may be used, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

Depending on how CuePilot is offered and used, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information, such as your name, email address, and sign-in details.
  • Content you add to CuePilot, such as cues, scripts, notes, show plans, and related production details.
  • Usage information, such as device type, browser type, pages viewed, and feature interactions.
  • Support communications, including messages you send when asking for help or reporting an issue.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide, maintain, and improve CuePilot. This includes:

  • Delivering the product features you request.
  • Saving and synchronizing your cue sheets, notes, and preferences.
  • Responding to support requests and communicating service updates.
  • Improving reliability, security, usability, and performance.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help operate CuePilot, when required by law, to protect the rights and safety of users, or as part of a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide CuePilot, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your account or personal information where applicable.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may be able to access, update, export, or delete certain information through your account settings. You may also contact us to ask questions about your information or privacy choices.

Children's Privacy

CuePilot is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the date above and provide additional notice where appropriate.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact CuePilot at privacy@cuepilot.app.
